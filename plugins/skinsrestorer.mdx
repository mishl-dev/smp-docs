Allows you to change your Minecraft skin without changing your account.

## Changing Your Skin

Change your appearance to any Minecraft skin, even if you're using an offline or different account.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Skin Commands" icon="palette">
    | Command | Description |
    |---------|-------------|
    | `/skin set <name>` | Set your skin to match another player |
    | `/skin url <url>` | Set your skin from a URL |
    | `/skin clear` | Reset to your default skin |
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Tips

<Tip>
  You can use any skin from any Minecraft account. The change is instant and visible to all players!
</Tip>

### Additional Tips

- Your skin persists between sessions
- Other players see your chosen skin immediately
- Change your skin as often as you want
- Use player names or direct image URLs
